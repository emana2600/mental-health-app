Anxiety

It is a sense of nervousness, tension and fear that our body has when it recognizes a threat. It is almost impossible to calm it down when it increases.

Some ways to calm it down can be:

Accepting it, if we accept what is happening to us can help us understand ourselves and if we have a fear of anxiety when we get anxious, it can lead to a worse outcome such as panic attacks. Taking on some relaxation techniques. When we face anxiety, our heart rate gets rapidly increased, our muscles get tightened and breathing becomes more rapid and slight. It can invoke nausea, lightheadedness and sweating which are included in the symptoms of anxiety. By using some relaxation techniques, managing our psychological and physical symptoms of anxiety becomes easier, it diverts our attention from the anxious thoughts. These techniques also allow us to make our mental and physical health better.
